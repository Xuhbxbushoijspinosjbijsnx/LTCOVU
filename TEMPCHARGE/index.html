Why am I being charged by LT.CO.VU/TEMPCHARGE?

​

You may notice a pending charge on your account. It is a temporary card verification hold coming from your bank to check your card’s validity and minimise any fraudulent use of your card and information.

​ This hold of money (authorisation) is never charged to your account, but it can be listed as a pending charge that is either returned immediately or within 10 business days, depending on your bank. If the amount has not been released after 10 business days, please contact your bank or financial institution. If you do not recognize this charge and have not bought anything from LT Hair Salon, please contact us at lthairsalon@gmail.com or call us at +65 6974 6742

​ Card verification hold can fail for the following reasons:

​ You have insufficient funds on your account

The system notices suspicious card activity.
